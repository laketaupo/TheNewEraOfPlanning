Only Item F (one of the three calibrated grades) continues downstream into Priming, where seeds are pre-conditioned to improve germination speed and uniformity. Items E and G branch off separately — their downstream paths are not shown here. The Primer machine consumes 2 hours per batch and produces Item H.
