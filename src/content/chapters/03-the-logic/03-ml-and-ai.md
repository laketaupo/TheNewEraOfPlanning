## AI as a Planning Augmentation Layer

o9 integrates machine learning throughout the planning workflow — not as a black box, but as a set of **explainable recommendations** that planners can accept, adjust, or override.

## Key AI Capabilities

### 1. Demand Sensing & Forecasting

o9 applies statistical and ML models (ARIMA, gradient boosting, neural networks) to:

- Generate baseline forecasts from historical sales
- Incorporate external signals (weather, promotions, macroeconomic data)
- Detect demand anomalies and one-off events
- Improve forecast accuracy at the item/location level

### 2. Inventory Optimization

ML models recommend safety stock levels based on:

- Demand variability
- Supply lead time variability
- Target service levels
- Cost of holding vs. stockout

### 3. Intelligent Alerts

Rather than surfacing every exception, o9 AI:

- Ranks exceptions by business impact
- Groups related exceptions (e.g., a single supplier delay causing 15 downstream alerts)
- Suggests the best corrective action

### 4. Decision Intelligence

For repetitive decisions (e.g., accept a supplier's counter-offer, reallocate inventory between DCs), o9 can surface an AI-recommended action that the planner approves with one click.

## Human in the Loop

All AI recommendations in o9 are **transparent and overridable**. The planner always sees the reasoning behind a recommendation and can reject it. Over time, the models learn from planner overrides to improve future suggestions.
