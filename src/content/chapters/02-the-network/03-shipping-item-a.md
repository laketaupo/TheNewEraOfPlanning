Two Ship processes run in parallel — one for Item A, one for Item B — moving both seed inputs from the Central Warehouse to the Grower via **Bill of Distribution (BOD)** lanes. BOD processes model transportation: the item itself doesn't change, only its location. Both items now appear at the Grower, ready to be consumed by Seed Production.
