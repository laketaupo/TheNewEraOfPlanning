The Coating BOM process applies a polymer film to the primed seeds, improving shelf life, protection, and traceability. Item H goes in; Item I — coated seed — comes out. The Coater machine runs at 1 hour per batch. Each BOM step adds value and specificity, making the item less interchangeable with upstream versions.
