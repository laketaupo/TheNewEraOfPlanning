A — Drill-down with breadcrumb

Each click replaces the current view with the next level's nodes. A breadcrumb trail at the top shows your path and lets you navigate back. Clean and focused — you only see one level at a time.

B — Expand in place

Clicking a node reveals its children directly below it, indented and connected. The parent stays visible. Multiple levels can be open at once. Good for seeing the full hierarchy at a glance, but can get tall.

C — Stacked levels (swimlanes)

All levels are shown as horizontal rows stacked vertically. Clicking a node filters and highlights the row below. You always see all active levels simultaneously. Dense but gives the full picture.
