What you're looking at

This is the Demand workspace in grid view, filtered to Cape Town DC over a 13-week horizon. Rows group items by product family — click the triangle to expand. Columns are planning weeks. Cell colour indicates status: green is on-target, amber is at risk, red is an active exception requiring review. The highlighted row (Cola 330ml) has a red cell in week 26 — this is the exception to investigate.

Steps

1
Open the Demand workspace from the left menu
2
Set the Location filter to your site
3
Set the Horizon to 13 weeks
4
Expand a product family row to see SKU-level data
5
Review any red cells — these are exceptions to investigate
6
Save the view with your filter settings for next time
